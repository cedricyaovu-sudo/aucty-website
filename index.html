FAQ

Questions, answered.

Every auction has a starting price and a timer. Tap "Place bid" to raise. If someone bids in the last 30 seconds, the timer auto-extends so nobody gets sniped. The highest bid when the timer hits zero wins.
Yes. Buyers never pay a fee. Sellers keep 92% of every sale — we charge a flat 8% commission with no listing fees, no subscriptions, no hidden costs.
Every purchase is covered by Aucty Protection. If the item doesn't match the listing, open a case within 7 days of delivery and we refund you automatically. No arguing, no proof-of-return loopholes.
Verification is free. Submit ID + a 30-second selfie video, and we review within 48 hours. Once approved, you get the blue check, higher listing limits, and access to live video auctions.
Currently the US, Canada, UK, EU, Australia, and Japan. Expansion to 20+ more countries is planned for 2026. Sellers can restrict shipping per listing.
